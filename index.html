Events

Letters For Love

May 1st, 2025 | Medford High School

Key Club members created heartfelt cards containing messages that were sent to hospitalized kids suffering from chronic, life-threatening diseases, with the goal of uplifting their spirits.

Rainbarrel Distribution

May 14th, 2025 | Medford High School

The City of Medford partnered with the Great American Rain Barrel Company to provide discounted rain barrels to Medford residents. Key Club members helped direct those who purchased rain barrels and loaded them onto their cars.

MHS Science Fair

Apr. 10th, 2025 | Medford High School

During MHS's yearly science fair, Key Club members help set up the science lecture hall and organize the room to provide a space for both contestants and judges.

Unitarian Universalist Church of Medford Food Drive

Feb. 27th - Mar. 27th, 2025 | MHS

Throughout the month of March, MHS Key Club members donated a plethora of non-perishable food items to the Unitarian Universalist Church of Medford's food pantry to help families in need.
